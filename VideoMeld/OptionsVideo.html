Video Section Settings

Menu Command: Options | Section Settings or right-click an video section and choose Settings or choose the Settings button in the section's title bar.

Use Video Section Settings to change the following:

  • Preview resolution
  • Text shown in the title bar
  • Time range of video played from the file
  • Playback speed
  • Colour of the section's title bar
Location
Shows the location on the hard drive where the audio file is stored. This setting cannot be changed.
Create Low Resolution Copy for Preview
Creates a smaller version of the video at a lower resolution so previewing is smoother and more responsive. Use this feature when working with high resolutions (4K) videos to reduce the processing overhead of previewing. The resolution of the preview copy is set by the Video preview quality setting under Options | Playback. Be sure to adjust that setting first. If you change that setting after creating a copy, you'll need to delete the copy and recreate it again.

Note that the high quality original video is always used when melding the final video. The low resolution copy is used for previewing only.

Delete Low Resolution Preview Copy
This button appears only after a preview copy is created. Use this button to delete the preview copy to free up storage or to always use the high quality original video for previewing.
Title
Sets the text shown in the title bar.
Range
Sets the start and finish times within the file. Choose the All button to play the entire file.
Speed
Sets the playback speed. Values above 100 increase the speed (fast motion). Values below 100 lower the speed (slow motion).

Use Video Effects to add special effects to the video, or to change it's size, position, and aspect ratio, or to adjust its transparency or colour.
