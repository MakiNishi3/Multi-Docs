Flanger

Menu Command: Effect | Add Or Modify, choose Add Effect [+*] button, choose Flanger

The Flanger effect uses variable delays and mixing to create some unusual audio effects, including echoes and reverbs. Settings can be entered manually or selected from presets.

Source Volume
Sets the volume of the sound that isn't processed by the Flanger effect. 100% is full volume. The sound may have been processed by other effects preceeding the Flanger effect.

Effect Volume
Sets the volume of the processed sound.

Feedback Volume
Sets the volume of feedback (the combination of the source, effect, and previous feedback).

Fixed Delay
Sets the fixed delay, which is a constant delay added to the variable delay.

Variable Delay
Set the maximum variable delay of the Flanger. A value of 0.04 varies the delay from 0 to 40 milliseconds.

Delay Frequency
Set the rate the delay is varied. A value of 2 varies the delay twice per second. A value of 0.2, varies the delay once every 5 seconds.

Frequency Modulator
Sets the function for varying the delay.

Volume Depth
Sets the range of volume change. A value of 80% causes the volume to range from 80% to 100%.

Depth Frequency
Sets the rate the volume is changed.

Depth Modulator
Sets the function for varying the volume.
