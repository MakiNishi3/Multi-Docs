Track Settings

Menu Command: Options | Track Settings or right-click a track and choose Track Settings

Use Track Settings to change the track's title, volume, mute, and solo settings.

Title
Sets the track's title.
Volume
Sets the volume for all audio sections on the track.
Mute, Solo, Normal
A muted track is excluded from playback and from melding. A solo track is played or melded exclusively and all other tracks are excluded.
