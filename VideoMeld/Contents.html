VideoMeld

VideoMeldTM is a fast, easy-to-use, multitrack audio and video editor. Use it to mix together many audio and video files into one file, replace or edit the audio or video tracks within a file, create presentations or YouTube videos, or add special effects to files.

Please see the ReadMe.txt file for additional information. The WhatsNew.txt file contains a list of changes. Updates and announcement are posted on the GoldWave website, Twitter, and Facebook.
